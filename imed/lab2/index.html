About the Dallas Womens Hiking Club

The Dallas Womens Hiking Club is a space based in Dallas, Texas, where women of all skill levels come together to explore nature, connect with each other, and empower themselves. We organize local hikes, overnight trips, and workshops on outdoor skills.
